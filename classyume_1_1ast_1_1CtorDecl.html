Detailed Description

A declaration of a custom constructor (def :new).

The arguments of a constructor may be regular TypeNames, just as in regular function declarations, but they also may be a FieldAccessExpr without a base (such as ::x), representing a shorthand where the field is set immediately, and the type is deduced to be that of the field. Essentially the following two declarations are identical:

struct Foo(data I32)
def :new(::data)
end
def :new(data I32)
::data = data
end
end
end
