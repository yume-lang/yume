Detailed Description

A function declaration in the compiler.

The primary use of this struct is to bind together the AST declaration of a function (ast::FnDecl) and the bytecode body of a function (llvm::Function). A function template may also be a Fn. Since a template doesn't actually have a body, its m_llvm_fn is nullptr. All the instantiations of a template are stored in m_instantiations.
